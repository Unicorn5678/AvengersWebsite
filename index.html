Avengers Website

The Avengers are a group of extraordinary individuals who were assembled to defeat Loki and his Chitauri army in New York City. Since then, the team has expanded and faced new threats.


Who are the main Avengers?

The main Avengers are: Captain America, Iron Man, Black Widow, Hulk, Thor and Hawkeye.


1.Captain America



Captain America is the alter ego of Steve Rogers, a frail young boy injected with an experimental "super-soldier serum" which made him strong. He joined the military to aid the United States government in World War II. Near the end of the war, he was trapped in ice until he was revived in modern times. Although Captain America is a man out of his time, he remains a highly respected figure in his community, which includes becoming the leader of the Avengers.


Real name: Steve Rogers


Portrayed by: Chris Evans


First Appearance: Marvel Comic #1






2. Iron Man



A wealthy American businessman, inventor and scientist, Tony Stark suffers from a severe chest injury during a kidnapping. When his kidnappers attempt to force him to build a weapon of mass destruction, he instead creates a mechanized suit of armor to save his life and escape. Later, Stark develops his suit, adding weapons and other technological devices he designed through his company, Stark Industries. He uses the suit to protect the world as Iron Man.


Real name: Tony Stark


Portrayed by: Robert Downey Jr.


First Appearance: Marvel Comic #39






3.Black Widow



Black Widow was introduced as a Russian spy, an antagonist of the superhero Iron Man. She later became an agent of the spy agency S.H.I.E.L.D. and a member of the Avengers.


Real name: Natasha Romanoff


Portrayed by: Scarlett Johansson


First Appearance: Marvel Comic #52






4. Hulk



The Hulk is a green-skinned and muscular humanoid possessing a vast degree of physical strength, and his alter ego is Dr.Bruce Banner, a physically weak and emotionally reserved physicist. Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk when he is angry but later on, he finds a way to combine both personalities.


Real name: Bruce Banner


Portrayed by: Mark Ruffalo


First Appearance: Marvel Comics - The Incredible Hulk #1






5. Thor



Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, the former king of Asgard and New Asgard, and a founding member of the Avengers. When his irresponsible and impetuous behavior reignited a conflict, Thor was denied the right to become king, stripped of his power, and banished to Earth by Odin.


Real name: Thor Odinson


Portrayed by: Chris Hemsworth


First Appearance: Marvel Comics - Venus #12






6. Hawkeye



Hawkeye is a skilled marksman, a former special agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. and one of the founding members of the Avengers. Known for his use of the bow and arrow as his primary weapon, Barton had become one of the best agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.


Real name: Clint Barton


Portrayed by: Jeremy Renner


First Appearance: Marvel Comics #57
